TM’s RM79 Unifi Basic plan is now open for pre-order


=============================================================

=============================================================
Block-210

Posted: 
July 15, 2018
 
By: 

=============================================================

=============================================================
Block-212
Alexander Wong

=============================================================

=============================================================
Block-214
17 comments

=============================================================

=============================================================
Block-224
To address the need for a cheaper broadband plan, TM has recently introduced its 

=============================================================

=============================================================
Block-226
Unifi Basic plan

=============================================================

=============================================================
Block-227
 that offers 30Mbps fibre broadband at RM79/month. If you’re interested, you can pre-order starting today and the plan will be available from 1st August 2018 onwards.

=============================================================

=============================================================
Block-231
To recap, Unifi Basic is a broadband only plan which gives you 30Mbps download and 10Mbps uploads. Unlike other Unifi plans, the quota is limited at 60GB a month and once you’ve hit your limit, the broadband speed will be throttled.

=============================================================

=============================================================
Block-235
In terms of eligibility, the plan is open to all Malaysians aged above 18 years old with a household income of less than RM4,500 (TM had mentioned RM4,500 and below during the announcement). You’ll need to provide supporting documents such as a verified salary slip, documents verified by local authorities or a copy of your BR1M acceptance letter. Each IC number is only eligible for one Unifi Basic Plan.

=============================================================

=============================================================
Block-237
The Unifi TV set-top-box is not included but you can watch all the channels for free using the 

=============================================================

=============================================================
Block-238
PlayTV@Unifi

=============================================================

=============================================================
Block-239
 app for the first 30 days. After that, you can still watch 17 channels that are offered for free.

=============================================================

=============================================================
Block-243
Although this is marketed as a broadband-only plan, you can still make voice calls at RM0.20/minute. TM isn’t bundling a free phone, but you can plug-in your own cordless phone.

=============================================================

=============================================================
Block-245
As usual, there’s a 24-month contract and there will be a penalty if you decide to terminate earlier. According to the 

=============================================================

=============================================================
Block-247
T&C

=============================================================

=============================================================
Block-248
, you are allowed to transfer the package to any third party during the contract period provided that the person meets the plan’s requirement.

=============================================================

=============================================================
Block-250
As a limited time promo, TM is also giving away a Unifi Liga Super jersey for free to those who pre-order from now until 14 August, while stocks last.

=============================================================

=============================================================
Block-254
For heavy users, this is obviously not the plan for you. Even for casual users, you might want to take note that TM isn’t offering any data booster add-ons. Once you’ve depleted your 60GB of quota, you’ll need to endure the throttled speeds until the next billing cycle. If you’re interested, the Unifi Basic plan is available exclusively on their 

=============================================================

=============================================================
Block-256
website

=============================================================

=============================================================
Block-257
 and you can learn more by checking out their 

=============================================================

=============================================================
Block-259
FAQ

=============================================================

=============================================================
Block-260
.

=============================================================

=============================================================
Block-262
For an affordable unlimited option, you could go for 

=============================================================

=============================================================
Block-264
Unifi Lite

=============================================================

=============================================================
Block-265
 that offers 10Mbps at RM129/month. The quota is unlimited and this plan will be 

=============================================================

=============================================================
Block-267
bumped up

=============================================================

=============================================================
Block-268
 to 100Mbps for free with the Turbo upgrade.

=============================================================

=============================================================
Block-270
TM Unifi Wireless

=============================================================

=============================================================
Block-272
If your area isn’t covered by Unifi Fibre, you could consider 

=============================================================

=============================================================
Block-274
Unifi Wireless

=============================================================

=============================================================
Block-275
 which also offers 60GB of quota at RM79/month. Unlike the wired Unifi Basic broadband plan, there’s no contract for this 4G broadband option but you’ll need to buy the subsidised Huawei Wireless Router at RM565. The device is easy to set up and it can support up to 64 wireless devices.

=============================================================

=============================================================
Block-279
After you’ve finished your 60GB quota, speeds will be throttled to 128kbps but you do have the option to buy as much as 20GB of data at RM40 to regain full speeds. To learn more, you can check out the 

=============================================================

=============================================================
Block-281
Unifi Wireless page.
